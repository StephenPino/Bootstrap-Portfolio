About Me

I was born in Dover, Delaware but have lived in Texas for 23 years -- so I'm practically a native Texan. I recieved my Bachelor of Science and Arts in Neuroscience from the University of Texas at Austin in the fall of 2016. I always strive to learn new things and I am particularly interested in subjects that are concerned with the brain, pharmaceuticals and computers. Outside of learning, I enjoy playing (and watching) basketball, playing video games and spending time with friends and family.

My interest in computers has led me to join the Coding Bootcamp at UT Austin, which I plan to use as my stepping-stone into the ever-expanding technology industry. Six months from now, I hope to be employed as a fullstack web developer with my newfound development skills refined and polished.
